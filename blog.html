Online Programs

-Introduction to Coding for Beginners:

Description: This course provides a beginner-friendly introduction to coding concepts, covering languages such as Python, JavaScript, and Scratch. Participants will learn the fundamentals of programming through hands-on exercises and projects. Topics Covered: Basic syntax, variables, data types, conditionals, loops, functions, and problem-solving techniques. Duration: 4 weeks (or customizable based on participants' needs). Prerequisites: None, suitable for beginners with no prior coding experience.

- Hackathon Bootcamp:

Description: This intensive bootcamp prepares participants to participate in hackathons and tech innovation challenges. Through workshops, team-building activities, and project-based learning, participants will learn essential skills in ideation, prototyping, project management, and presentation. Topics Covered: Ideation techniques, rapid prototyping, collaboration tools, project management methodologies, pitching skills, and teamwork. Duration: 2 days to 1 week (intensive format). Prerequisites: Basic understanding of programming concepts and willingness to collaborate in a team setting.

-STEM Entrepreneurship:

Description: This course explores the intersection of STEM education and entrepreneurship, equipping participants with the knowledge and skills needed to launch tech startups. Through case studies, guest lectures, and hands-on projects, participants will learn about market research, business model canvas, product development, and pitching. Topics Covered: Market analysis, customer validation, lean startup methodology, product-market fit, fundraising, and scaling. Duration: 8 weeks (or customizable based on participants' needs). Prerequisites: Basic understanding of STEM concepts and interest in entrepreneurship.

-Mobile App Development for Education:

Description: This course focuses on developing mobile apps for school communication and organization. Participants will learn app development concepts and tools, including user interface design, database integration, push notifications, and cross-platform development. Topics Covered: Mobile app architecture, UI/UX design principles, backend development, API integration, testing, and deployment. Duration: 6 weeks (or customizable based on participants' proficiency level). Prerequisites: Basic programming knowledge (e.g., HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and familiarity with mobile devices and applications.

-Digital Literacy for Seniors:

Description: This course aims to improve digital literacy among seniors or underserved communities, covering essential skills such as using computers, navigating the internet, online safety, and communication tools. Topics Covered: Basic computer operations, internet browsing, email, online security, social media, and online banking. Duration: 4 weeks (or customizable based on participants' needs). Prerequisites: None, suitable for seniors with little to no prior experience with technology.
